Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using your phone number (SMS) or email address. We'll send you a verification code to confirm your identity.

How does voting work?

When an event is active, you can select your top 2-3 pizza choices and rank them by preference. First choice gets 3 points, second gets 2 points, and third gets 1 point. The app calculates the best pizzas to order based on everyone's votes.

Can I change my vote?

Yes! You can update your vote anytime before the voting deadline. Just go to the Vote tab and make your changes.

Why does the app need my location?

Location access is only used for the "Find Pizza Places" feature to show you nearby pizza restaurants. You can deny this permission and still use all other features of the app.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below. We'll process your request within 30 days.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Make sure you have a stable internet connection, 2) Close and reopen the app, 3) Update to the latest version. If problems persist, contact us for help.
